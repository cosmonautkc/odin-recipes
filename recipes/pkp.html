How to make Pad Kra Pao

Description

A personal favourite dish, made with the flavours of Thailand including; birdseye chillis, palm sugar and basil. Served with a crispy fried egg.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of mince meat(choose your meat) or chicken
  • 3 shallots
  • 8 cloves of garlic
  • 4 tbsp of fish sauce
  • 4 tbsp of thick soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp of oyster sauce
  • 4 tbsp of palm sugar
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 3 Birdseye chillis
  • A dash of MSG
  • A small sprinkle of salt(to taste)
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Prepare shallots, garlic (and chicken if chosen)
      Dice the shallots and finely chop the garlic. If you chose to use chicken, cut it into small chunks, you can break them up more in
      the pan to cook faster, so don't worry about their initial size.
  2. Fry the ingredients
      Add some butter to your pan, then add the shallots and stir on low heat, once they become a translucent, add the garlic and keep
      frying until you can see the garlic turn a deeper colour, slice the chillis and add to the pan stirring until fragrant.
  3. Add the meat to the pan
      Add and combine the meat with the ingredients in the pan, stir and turn the heat up to medium.
  4. Add sauces to the pan
      Once the meat is 80% cooked, add the fish sauce, thick soy sauce and oyster sauce to the pan and combine with ingredients.
  5. Chop the basil
      Once chopped, you can add this to the pan, and stir it into the sauce.
  6. Add sugar, MSG and salt
      Add these ingredients to taste, depending on whether you want a sweeter sauce, more of a salty sauce or an even mix. If you prefer
      more sauce, add some water and let this reduce for 5-7 minutes.
  7. Fry your egg
      In another pan, add butter and turn the heat up to medium/high, once heated, crack an egg and fry until it has a crispy brown bottom.
  8. Plate your food up
      And enjoy! Best served with a rice base.
