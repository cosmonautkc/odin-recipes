How to make Crispy Belly Pork

Description

An easy to cook, all time favourite dish, Crispy Belly Pork. Made with the flavours of Beautiful Thailand and in the air fryer - quick and easy.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of Belly Pork
  • 8 cloves of Garlic
  • 3 tbsp of Fish sauce
  • 4 tbsp of Thick soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp of Oyster sauce
  • 4 Birdseye Chillis
  • 4 tbsp of Palm Sugar
  • A sprinkle of MSG
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Prepare the pork, garlic and Chilli
      Cut the pork into small chunks, finely chop the garlic and thinly slice the chillis.
  2. Prepare the marinade
      In a large bowl, combine the finely chopped garlic, fish sauce, thick soy sauce, oyster sauce, chopped bird‘s eye chilies, palm sugar,
      MSG, and salt. Stir well until the pork chunks are all covered in the marinade and evenly coated with all the ingredients.
  3. Marinate the pork
      Add the chunks of pork belly to the bowl. Toss well to coat every piece evenly in the marinade. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30
      minutes, or up to 4 hours for deeper flavor.
  4. Cook the pork
      Place the marinated pork into the air fryer, making sure the chunks are evenly spread. Avoid overcrowding for max crisp.
      Cook for 20 - 25 minutes, flipping half way through until the pork is golden and crispy on the outside.
  5. Eat and Enjoy
      This dish can be served with rice, nahm prik and even a fried egg or on its own, whatever you choose!
